About Us

We are here, Nothing to fear


Welcome to Bishop Heber College's Fine Arts Department, where creativity meets excellence! Our vibrant and dynamic community is dedicated to fostering a passion for the arts and nurturing the talents of our students. At Bishop Heber, we believe in the transformative power of artistic expression, and our Fine Arts Department serves as a hub for innovation, inspiration, and collaboration. Committed to providing a comprehensive and enriching artistic education, our department boasts a diverse range of programs and courses that empower students to explore, experiment, and excel in various artistic disciplines. From visual arts to performing arts, our talented faculty and students continuously push the boundaries of creativity. Join us on this exciting journey of self-discovery, artistic exploration, and the celebration of boundless imagination. Together, let's paint a canvas of endless possibilities and create a symphony of artistic brilliance!

Identity Crew

Introducing Identity Crew, our college's powerhouse Western dance team, renowned for their dominance on both national and state stages. With a name that resonates excellence, Identity Crew consistently raises the bar with their mesmerizing performances, captivating audiences with their precision and passion. Their victories in countless competitions stand as a testament to their unparalleled talent and unwavering dedication. As ambassadors of our college, they embody the spirit of unity and creativity, inspiring all who witness their electrifying routines. With each graceful movement and synchronized step, Identity Crew leaves an indelible mark, solidifying their legacy as champions in the world of dance

Musical Team

Introducing Crescendo Harmony, our college's renowned musical ensemble, captivating audiences with their diverse repertoire and unmatched skill. With a string of victories in national and state-level competitions, they are revered for their innovative interpretations and captivating performances. Led by visionary conductors, they seamlessly blend classical elegance with contemporary flair, leaving a lasting impression on all who hear them. Their harmonious arrangements and cultural vibrancy showcase the transformative power of music, inspiring aspiring musicians within our college community. Crescendo Harmony stands as a beacon of artistic excellence, pushing the boundaries of creativity and leaving an indelible mark on the musical landscape.

MIME Team

Introducing Silent Expressions, our college's mesmerizing mime team, captivating audiences with their wordless storytelling on national and state-level stages. Led by visionary directors, they convey profound narratives through emotive movements, showcasing the power of expression without uttering a single word. Silent Expressions fosters creativity and collaboration, inspiring aspiring artists within our college community. With each performance, they redefine the art of mime, leaving an unforgettable impression on spectators and peers alike.
